TypeScript 5 with ESLint 9 and Prettier 3. Frontend uses React 19 + MUI v7 + Zustand + i18next; follow unified design tokens, avoid environment-dependent characters or emojis in code/comments, keep layout spacing/padding consistent. IPC channels are defined in `src/shared/types.ts`, handlers in `src/main/ipc/index.ts`, preload bridges in `src/preload/channels.ts`, and renderer callers under `src/renderer`. Config changes must update `src/shared/constants.ts` and be managed through `ConfigManager`. Dynamic imports are used for ngrok and selfsigned to maintain ESM/CJS compatibility.
